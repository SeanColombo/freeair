# Privacy Policy
Last updated: July 19, 2026

FreeAir does not collect, store, or share any personal information on any server.

The app does not require you to create an account and does not track your activity.

The app retrieves publicly available air quality data from PurpleAir based on the sensor(s) you
select. No personal information is sent to the developer.

Your PurpleAir API key and sensor ID(s) are stored locally on your device only, so the app can
fetch your sensor's data. They are never sent to us or to anyone other than PurpleAir's own API.

If this app's data practices change in the future, this privacy policy will be updated.
